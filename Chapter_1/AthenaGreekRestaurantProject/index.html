Welcome!

Are you ready to dine like a Greek God? We really hope so! We warmly welcome you to Athena Greek Restuarant, our family owned place, where we serve you traditional Greek food of the highest quality. And yes, after you try our food, you will feel like dining with the Gods at Mount Olympus.

Check out our menu

Gyro Plate

Made from lamb meat cooked on a vertical rotisserie. Served with pita bread, Greek yogurt, French fries, and a house salad.

Gyro Wrap

A true classic. Lamb meat cooked to perfection combined with tomatos, onions, Greek Yogurt and spices. Served with french fries.

Greek Salad Choriatiki

Made with pieces of tomatoes, sliced cucumbers, onion, feta cheese, and olives. Seasoned with salt and Greek mountain oregano, and dressed with olive oil.

Spanikopita

The traditional filling comprises chopped spinach, feta cheese, onions or spring onions, egg, and seasoning.

Grekiska koket

Melted cheese in a small frying pan until it is bubbling and generally served with lemon juice and pepper.

Swordfish in Limnionas

Our signiture dish! Baked swordfish with cilantro, lemon, tomatoes, zuchini, zucchini and baked potatoes.
